Clock

This project aims to poetically present the concept of time. I have designed a "calendar" comprising 12 squares, each representing a month of the year, symbolized by a characteristic flower. When you click on each flower grid, a new grid with 30 squares appears, representing the days of the month. A dot in a square signifies that a flower blooms on that day. More dots indicate a greater number of flowers in bloom, with days showing the most dots being peak bloom days. However, this pattern does not apply to all flower types. Some flowers bloom for months at a stretch, so during certain months, you will see a continuous bloom from the start to the end of the month.
